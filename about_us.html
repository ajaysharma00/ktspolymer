ABOUT K.T.S. POLYMERS

KTS Polymers has a clear vision and in-depth understanding of the needs of the various sectors of plastic industry. The company established itself in 2004 as a Manufacturer of Masterbatch, Thermoplastic Rubber Compounds, TPEs Compounds and other allied. We offer quality compounds that display excellent properties to achieve coveted results. With our compounding ability, expertise and modern facilities, we help you accomplish superior quality and process-ability. We have the right solution for your specific as well as comprehensive requirements. Our range is widely recognised due to its value engineered and cost effective concepts.

Excellent in quality, our offered Thermoplastic Rubber Compounds and colored masterbatches are applicable in various industrial applications. These products find their use in household, automotive, electrical & electronics, footwear, packaging,molding and plastic industries. Our motive has been to strive in the direction of success by committing ourselves to growth & expansion. Over a period of time, the firm has grown capacities.

We are committed to give our customers, total satisfaction in terms of quality, cost and on-time delivery. We shall pursue continual improvement in product quality by upgrading the technology.

All our masterbatches and tpr compound are produced on state of the art high torque Co-rotating Twin Screw Extruders.

Twin Screw Extruders are known for producing highest quality masterbatches with their separate elements for mixing, kneading and conveying actions, thus ensuring consistency and high dispersion efficiency.

Our organization is growing with valuable assistance of Mr. Aman Gupta. His management skills, ability to handle crucial situation and regular motivation, enabled us to achieve success in the industry.

About Us
